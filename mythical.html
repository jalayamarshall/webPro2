The Mythical Man-Month: Essays on Software Engineering (Anniversary Edition)

In this legendary collection of essays, Fred Brooks explores the challenges and pitfalls of large-scale software development—from project planning and scheduling to the limits of human collaboration. Best known for the adage “adding manpower to a late software project makes it later,” this book remains a foundational read for developers and team leads alike.

Author: Frederick P. Brooks Jr.
Published: 1995 (Anniversary Edition)
Genre: Software Engineering, Tech History
